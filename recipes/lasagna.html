Lasagna

Description

The most delicious lasagna recipe you will ever make!

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta sheets
  • marinara sauce
  • ground beef
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and set aside
  2. Heat up the marinara sauce and set aside
  3. Begin layering the sheets with beef, cheese, and marinara sauce
  4. layer the top with extra cheese
  5. Bake at 375F for 40 minutes
